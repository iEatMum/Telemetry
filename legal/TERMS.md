## The service

Telemetry — The Discipline Ledger is a self-tracking and planning tool. You
get a local ledger for your own commitments; a paid subscription adds
AI-generated coaching content ("the coach").

## Not medical care

Telemetry is **not** a medical device, therapy, treatment, or a substitute for
professional care. Its optional recovery-oriented features are self-management
tools only. If you are struggling with addiction, self-harm, or a mental
health crisis, contact a qualified professional — in the US, call or text 988
(Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), or find local services at findahelpline.com.

The optional **AI health read** summarizes patterns in the health metrics you
choose to share (sleep, activity, heart data, and similar). It is general
wellness information only — never a diagnosis, screening, treatment
recommendation, or medical advice, and it must not be relied on as such. Do
not use Telemetry to make medical decisions; consult a clinician for any
health concern.

## AI content

Coaching text may be generated by an AI model. It can be wrong, generic, or a
poor fit for your situation. It is suggestions about scheduling and habits —
never medical, legal, or financial advice. You decide what to act on.

## Subscription

The coach layer is sold as an auto-renewing subscription ($6.99/month or
$39.99/year after a 7-day free trial), billed through your Apple account.
Manage or cancel in iOS Settings → Subscriptions; cancellation takes effect at
the end of the current period. The free tier (the book) stays functional
without a subscription.

## Your content

Your entries are yours and stay on your device (see PRIVACY.md). You are
responsible for keeping your device secure and for exports you create.

## Acceptable use

Don't reverse-engineer the app, resell access, or use it to harm yourself or
others. We may discontinue features or the app itself; because data is local,
your ledger and exports survive us.
