The lane-red grammar

Lane-red marks COMMITMENT only: Start, the seal, the ◆ high-impact marker, the active HELP state. It can never mark a miss — a miss is withheld ink (muted, dashed), never a stain. accent-text is the AA-passing variant every piece of red TEXT wears (≥4.5:1 on surface-2); surfaces and seals keep the true accent. pos/neg/warn are reserved for EXTERNAL data (biometrics, conditions) — never a person's behavior.
